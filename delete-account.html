Delete Your Account

How to permanently remove your account and all associated data from Lifyinos.

How to Delete Your Account in the App

You can delete your account directly inside the Lifyinos app in a few steps. No email or web form is required.

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile tab (bottom-right).
  2. Tap Settings.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Select a reason for leaving (required), then tap Delete My Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.
  6. A 6-digit verification code will be sent to your registered email address. Enter it to confirm your identity.
  7. Your account is permanently deleted. You will be signed out automatically.

What Data Is Deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of the following data from Lifyinos:

  • Your profile (name, email address, phone number)
  • All tasks, goals, and daily logs you have created
  • Voice input transcripts and daily journal entries
  • Health data synced from HealthKit or Health Connect (steps, workouts, sleep)
  • AI-generated insights and day summaries
  • Inner Circle connections, reactions, and comments
  • All account preferences and settings

Data Retention

Your account and data are deleted immediately when you complete the verification step. Any residual copies in encrypted system backups are fully purged within 30 days.
